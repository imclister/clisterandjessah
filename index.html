To My Dearest

moment with you is a bloom in the garden of my life. Like a tulip that opens its petals to the morning sun, my heart opens to you with warmth, trust, and an endless longing. I’ve found in you the poetry I never knew I was meant to write, the gentle rhythm in the chaos of my days.

Though the world may change and seasons may fade, my love for you stands timeless. You are the reason I smile without cause, hope without fear, and dream without end. In every heartbeat, you are there—my forever, my always.
